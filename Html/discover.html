Sri Lanka


Sri Lanka is a beautiful, exotic destination full of culture, nature, wildlife, and smiling faces. For a country with such a violent (and recently so, at that) history, the island is actually home to some of the friendliest people out there. Located between India and southeast Asia, Sri Lanka is neither quite like India nor Asia, leaving it in somewhat of a curious middle ground as far as geography, features, culture, landscape, and customs.

Amazing food

Making liberal use of local fruit, such as coconut and jackfruit, seafood and an arsenal of spices, Sri Lankan cooking delivers an abundance of incredible dishes.

Frendly people's

Many foreigners that visit often or stay on the island for a long time fall in love with its people pretty quickly and may also start new families Love Sri lanka.

Traditional

Sri Lankan culture has long been influenced by the heritage of Theravada, and the religion's legacy is particularly in Sri Lanka's southern and central regions.

Cylon Gem

Sri Lanka is the top source of finest Sapphires to the global market and exports Blue Sapphire, star sapphires, and range of fancy sapphires to the global gem markets.

A tropical climate all year round

Sri Lanka's climate is tropical and consists of distinct wet and dry seasons. Generally, In the coastal areas of Sri Lanka enjoy temperatures averaging 28°C (82°F).
